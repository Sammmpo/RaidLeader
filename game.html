ID Personality DPS Gear Spirit Time

    Personalities:
  • 0 = Not in Group
  • 1 = Raid Leader (player)
  • 2 = Common (No special features)
  • 3 = Loot Hoarder (Takes 10 Drama if doesn't get Loot.)
  • 4 = Guild Officer (Won't leave at 0 Spirit. Can't be kicked.) *COMING SOON*
  • 5 = Butthurt (+100% Drama taken)
  • 6 = Noob (-50% DPS)
    Loot:
  • Rolling Loot gives a random member +20 Gear and +20 Spirit.
  • Gifting Loot lets you choose the member, but deals 5 Drama to everyone else.
    Leader Abilities:
  • Cheer (toggle): Stop DPS and increase everyone's Spirit 1 per second (up to 100 Spirit).
  • Rage (toggle): All DPS is increased by 100%, but deal 1 Drama per second to all other members.
    Stats:
  • DPS: Amount of damage dealt to the boss each second (Gear * Spirit).
  • Gear: Increased with Loot.
  • Spirit: Drama reduces Spirit. When Spirit drops to 0, the member Rage Quits.
  • Time: Consumed each second. When Time drops to 0, the member leaves.
