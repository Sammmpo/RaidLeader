ID Personality DPS Gear Spirit Time Active

    Personalities:
  • 0 = Not in Group
  • 1 = Raid Leader (player)
  • 2 = Common (No special features)
  • 3 = Loot Hoarder (Takes 10 Drama if doesn't get Loot.)
  • 4 = Kid (-50% Time)
  • 5 = Butthurt (+100% Drama taken)
  • 6 = Noob (-50% DPS)
    Actions
  • Roll: Spend -10 Loot to give a random member +20 Gear and +20 Spirit.
  • Gift: Spend -10 Loot to give the targeted member +20 Gear and +20 Spirit. Deal 5 Drama to all other members.
  • Kick: Remove the targeted member from the raid.
  • Cheer (toggle): All DPS is -100%. Boost all member's Spirit 1 per second (up to 100 Spirit).
  • Rage (toggle): All DPS is +100%. Deal 1 Drama per second to all members.
  • Pull: Start dealing damage to the enemy.
  • LFM: Spend 60 Time to find a random member to the selected raid slot.
    Events
  • Rage Quit: Deal 10 Drama to all members.
  • ***Distraction, Disconnection, Toxic Cloud, Salt***
    Stats:
  • DPS: Amount of damage dealt to the boss each second (Gear * Spirit).
  • Gear: Increased with Loot.
  • Spirit: Drama reduces Spirit. When Spirit drops to 0, the member leaves and triggers "Rage Quit".
  • Time: Consumed each second. When Time drops to 0, the member leaves.
